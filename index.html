The Artist

Annie He is a Digital Artist and Studio Art Practice Major. She lives in San Jose, California and is a first-generation Chinese American. Her art style consists mainly of anime style which many professional teachers disregard as "art". Annie hopes to push the boundaries using this art style to create new artworks and projects. She hopes to use her concept character design artworks to create new characters for future video games and create her own narratives and lore. She continues to learn, practice, and perfect her art every day. She is currently applying for the DMA major in hopes of involving her art into the video game field of DMA.

Experienced in Medibang Paint Pro, Fire Alpaca, Clip Art Studio, and Photoshop.
