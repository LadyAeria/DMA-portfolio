Artist Statement

My work mainly revolves around digital 2D illustrations and concept art for characters. My art style is anime-influenced as anime was a big eye-opener when it came to art and animation, yet professional teachers don't consider anime as an art because it's "too easy" which baffles me. If someone considered a pair of glasses accidentally placed on the floor "art", what makes anime any less? With my art style, I'm hoping people can open up to new forms of art because there are many types that some people not aware of. The drawings I usually draw are portraits, concept designs, fan arts, and submissions for art challenges. I dream of working for video game companies to create concept designs and characters because video games transfer you to another universe. I find fictional characters very immersive and like many characters people create, they take on a personality of their own which people love or hate. I want to create stories following around a character in a fictional fantasy world that people can enjoy and even relate to. I want to be able to make amazing lore and new characters that people have not been able to experience. Video games themselves have moral messages that people don’t realize, and the stories created within these platforms are breathtaking whether they are just for laughs and games or deep and meaningful. I want to create a character, whether they are a protagonist, antagonist, or side character, and have people enjoy their unique design and personality. The overall goal for me as an artist is to create. I hope to learn and create concept arts and designs for characters in future games, to create an immersive story with unique characters, and I want to have people have fun and enjoy in general and lose themselves as they get transported into another universe.

– Annie He
